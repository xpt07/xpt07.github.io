About Me

I am Guilherme Noronha Jardim, a games engineer focused on C++ programming, computer graphics, and physics simulation. I am completing an MSc in Games Engineering at the University of Warwick, following a First-Class BSc in Games Programming from De Montfort University.

My work spans a custom 2D physics engine (rigid-body dynamics, collision & impulses), an extended ray tracer with BVH and PBR materials, and leadership of a team Unreal project with physics-driven gameplay, modular systems and reactive audio.

I care about real-time performance and engineering clarity—from a 40% rasterizer speed-up to multithreaded networking tools. I aim to contribute to game engine and advanced simulation problems that blend creativity with rigor.
